My Story

Jolene Prins 11th February 2023 1 Comments

In 2010, Jolene moved to India, immersing herself in the culture, learning Hindi, and travelling with her three young daughters to every corner of that beautiful and fascinating country, hungry to uncover its secrets.

While there, she co-founded and became managing director of brand-consulting and marketing agency THEY VISUALIZE VALUE in New Delhi who help visionary entrepreneurs and companies tell their stories through messaging, images, and impactful headlines. Since then, she has also helped establish a branch of THEY in Amsterdam in 2016.

As managing director, she is responsible for all processes, coordinates her team, communicates with clients, and keeps all aspects of the business flowing smoothly and efficiently. In addition to creating content for annual reports, websites, corporate videos and internal magazines for companies, Jolene enjoys writing about topics that shape her everyday life in Amsterdam and New Delhi.

She realised that during her time in India, she had collected a number of stories that she wanted to tell. Inspired to help others through sharing her stories, she entered a four-year programme in writing and literature studies at the Scriptplus language centre at the Vrije Universiteit (Amsterdam) and continued studying at the Schrijversacademy.

Now, back in The Netherlands, dividing her time between Amsterdam and Delhi, her extended periods in India have allowed her to experience local culture with a depth and intimacy that is afforded few expats. Her close Indian friends brought her closer to the heart of Indian culture. With her experience, she hopes to help other non-Indians build a bridge between their cultural backgrounds and India through blogs. She aims to offer the same for Indian expats in the Netherlands with a second blog dedicated to getting the most out of life in the Low Countries.

For her, the journey is always just beginning, and around every corner is a new story she is eager to live and to tell.
