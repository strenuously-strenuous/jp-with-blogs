Blog

Twelve years surrounded by Indian culture, Indian friends and Indian colleagues made me beyond being Dutch. Living in India for the first six years and then travelling in and out for my work for another six years made coming back to the Netherlands not coming home but it was more “starting over”. As in an adventure, finding a way again. Being someone else and yet again not. What does that mean? What does it feel like? And what am I experiencing? I like to share it with you in my blogs.
